About us

Travel to Peru! is a community of travelers that share their experiences! Places to discover, local communities to visit, guides to hire or even companies, Travel to Peru! is providing a wide range of advices for travelers based on what they are looking for.

Our founder

Arthur left for Peru when he was only 20. After a few weeks of acclimating to a new lifestyle, the need to travel around the country became urgent. Throughout his trips, he created bounds with local guides and communities and shared them with other tourists to maximize their traveling experience in Peru. After coming back, Arthur founded this platform to share his traveling tips and recommendations.
